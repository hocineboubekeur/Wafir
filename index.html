Who We Are & Why Wafir

Rescuing Food.
Feeding Communities.

Every year, nearly 30% of food in Algeria is wasted — while food prices keep rising. Wafir bridges the gap between surplus and need.

📍 The Problem in Algiers

Restaurants, bakeries, supermarkets & hotels dispose of
perfectly edible food daily — with no digital outlet.

There is no efficient digital redistribution system in Algeria. Wafir solves this by connecting consumers directly with trusted Wafir Retailer accounts listing surplus food as Surprise Bags at the end of service — at 40–60% off retail price. Unclaimed bags are redirected to charities for free.

Risk Management

Pitfalls to Avoid

Anticipating risks before they arise is what separates a lasting startup from a cautionary tale.

🐢
Premature Scaling
Resist the urge to expand to multiple cities before the Algiers model is battle-tested. Perfect operations locally, then scale with confidence.
⚠️ Growth Risk
🚚
Charity Logistics Failure
Tight pickup windows require reliable last-mile delivery. We mitigate this with subsidised Yassir delivery partnerships for unclaimed bag redistribution.
⚠️ Ops Risk
🍽️
Food Safety Compliance
Onboarding unvetted retailers risks trust. We enforce strict listing rules, mandatory retailer training, and regular compliance audits across the network.
⚠️ Safety Risk
🔐
Weak Data Security
User trust is everything. Wafir implements GDPR-level encryption and stores zero raw payment data — compliance from day one, not as an afterthought.
⚠️ Security Risk
🌾
Allergen Mismanagement
A single allergen incident destroys credibility. We enforce mandatory allergen training, dual-labelling on all bags, and regular retailer audits to protect consumers.
⚠️ Health Risk
💸
Underpricing the Model
Setting discounts too aggressively makes the platform unsustainable. Our Financial Mathematics team models break-even thresholds to keep discount tiers viable for all parties.
⚠️ Financial Risk
